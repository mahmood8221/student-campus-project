Spring Festival Highlights

A. AlBahraini

Published on April 10, 2025

Students enjoying the spring festival activities

The annual Spring Festival brought together over 2,000 students for a day filled with cultural celebrations, live music, and community building.

Event Highlights

The festival grounds were divided into three main areas:

  • Cultural Pavilion: Featuring traditional foods and performances from 15 different countries
  • Main Stage: Live bands and student performances throughout the day
  • Activity Zone: Interactive games and team-building exercises

Community Impact

Many participants commented on the inclusive atmosphere and opportunity to connect with peers from different faculties.
